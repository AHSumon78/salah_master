# Salah Master - Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026

Salah Master is a prayer time, jamaat alarm, general alarm, auto silent, qibla finder, mosque locator, Hijri calendar, dua/azkar, and Islamic utility app. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses, why it is used, and how your privacy is handled.

In this policy, "we", "our", or "the app" refers to Salah Master.

## Information We Use

Salah Master may use the following types of information to provide its core features:

- Location data: your current location, saved home/office/special locations, mosque geofences, qibla direction, nearby mosques, and location-based prayer/jamaat alarms.
- Background location: used to detect travel, update nearby mosques, register geofences, and enable auto silent/DND mode when entering or leaving mosque areas.
- Alarm and preference data: prayer alarms, jamaat alarms, general alarms, repeat days, snooze settings, auto-stop, pre-alarm settings, vibration, gradual volume, selected sound, theme, and language.
- Audio/media access: used only when you select a system alarm sound or a custom audio file for alarms.
- Device sensors: compass and accelerometer sensors may be used for the qibla compass.
- Notification and alarm permissions: used to show timely alarms, pre-alarm notifications, and full-screen alarm screens on the lock screen.
- Do Not Disturb access: used to enable silent/DND mode for mosques, prayer times, or manual silent timers, and to restore the normal phone mode afterward.
- Battery optimization access: requested to improve alarm accuracy and background reliability.

## How We Use Information

We use the information only to provide app features, including:

- Calculating prayer times based on your location.
- Managing different jamaat alarms for different locations.
- Applying the correct alarm settings when you move between home, office, or other saved locations.
- Finding nearby mosques, showing them on a map, and opening directions.
- Enabling silent/DND mode when you enter a mosque geofence and restoring normal mode when you exit.
- Showing qibla direction and approximate distance to the Kaaba.
- Saving Hijri calendar, Islamic event, widget, theme, and language preferences.

## Where Data Is Stored

Most app data is stored locally on your device, including alarms, saved locations, mosques, settings, language/theme preferences, and widget data. The app is not designed to upload your personal data to our own server.

## Third-Party Services

Some features may use third-party services. These services may receive your IP address, location coordinates, or request data when you use related features:

- Google Play Services Location: for device location and geofencing.
- OpenStreetMap tile servers: for displaying maps.
- Overpass API: for finding nearby mosques.
- Google Maps: when you open map search or directions, location data may be sent to Google Maps.
- Aladhan API: for Hijri date and Islamic calendar related information.

These third-party services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal information with advertising networks. Required requests may be sent to the third-party services listed above to provide app features.

## Background Location

Salah Master may use background location when:

- Location-based jamaat alarms are enabled.
- Mosque geofence or auto silent/DND features are enabled.
- Nearby mosques need to be updated and registered for geofencing.

Without background location, these features may not work reliably. You can disable location permission at any time from Android settings.

## What Happens If Permissions Are Disabled

- If location permission is disabled, prayer time calculation, qibla, mosque locator, geofencing, and location-based alarms may be limited or unavailable.
- If notification/alarm permissions are disabled, alarms may not ring on time or may not appear as full-screen alerts.
- If DND access is disabled, auto silent features will not work.
- If audio/media permission is disabled, custom sound selection may be limited.
- If battery optimization is enabled, alarms may be delayed on some devices.

## Children's Privacy

Salah Master does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child's personal information has been stored by mistake, please contact us.

## Data Deletion

You can delete saved app data by:

- Deleting alarms, locations, or mosque entries inside the app.
- Clearing app storage/data from Android settings.
- Uninstalling the app.

For privacy or data deletion support, contact us using the details below.

## Security

We use local device storage and Android's permission system to help protect your information. However, no method is 100% secure, so please avoid storing sensitive personal information in the app.
